Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

Introduction

ClickSolveTools ("we," "us," "our") operates the website clicksolvetools.com and is committed to protecting the privacy of every person who uses our tools. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect — and, more importantly, what we do not collect — when you visit our site or use any of our 85 free browser-based tools.

The Short Version

We don't collect your data. All tools run entirely in your browser. Nothing you type, upload, or generate is ever sent to our servers.

Data We Do NOT Collect

Every tool on ClickSolveTools is powered by client-side JavaScript. Processing happens on your device, and we have no mechanism to intercept, receive, or store the following:

  • File contents — images, PDFs, documents, audio files, or any other media you open in our tools.
  • Text inputs — passwords, JSON payloads, SQL queries, markdown, code snippets, or any other text you enter.
  • Calculation inputs — financial figures, health metrics, math expressions, or any numerical data you provide.
  • Generated outputs — UUIDs, hashes, QR codes, barcodes, converted files, or any results our tools produce for you.

Data We May Collect

While we do not collect personal data through our tools, certain third-party services we rely on may collect limited, anonymized information:

Google Analytics 4

We may use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) with Consent Mode v2 to understand aggregate traffic patterns such as page views, approximate geographic region, device type, and browser. GA4 uses first-party cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information in our configuration. No advertising features are enabled. You can opt out at any time by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cloudflare Analytics

Our site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. As part of serving web traffic, Cloudflare may collect basic request metadata — including IP addresses in server logs — which are automatically purged in accordance with Cloudflare's privacy policy and data retention schedule.

Error Logs

We may collect anonymous, aggregated crash and error data (such as JavaScript error messages and stack traces) to diagnose issues and improve tool reliability. These logs do not contain any user input or personally identifiable information.

Cookies

Google Analytics 4 may set first-party cookies (such as _ga and _ga_*) to distinguish unique visitors and sessions. We do not use third-party advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or tracking cookies from social media platforms. You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

ClickSolveTools relies on the following third-party services, each of which operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Cloudflare — hosting, CDN, and DDoS protection (privacy policy).
  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymized, aggregate traffic analytics (privacy policy).
  • CDN libraries — we load open-source libraries from jsdelivr.net and cdnjs.cloudflare.com. These CDN providers may log HTTP requests in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Local Storage

Some tools may use your browser's localStorage to remember your preferences — for example, unit settings, theme selection, or the last values you entered in a calculator. This data is stored exclusively on your device, is never transmitted to any server, and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

ClickSolveTools does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Because our tools do not require accounts, logins, or any form of data submission, there is no mechanism through which a child (or any user) would provide personal information to us. If you believe that a child has somehow provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take prompt steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of ClickSolveTools after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to us at contact@clicksolvetools.com. We will do our best to respond promptly.
