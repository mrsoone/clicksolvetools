How It Works

Every tool on ClickSolveTools runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. When you paste text, upload an image, or enter numbers into a calculator, your data is processed right on your device — nothing is sent to a server, nothing is stored in a database, and nothing is logged. There's no account system because there's nothing to track. The site is hosted on Cloudflare's global edge network, so pages load fast from anywhere in the world. Open a tool, use it, and move on.
